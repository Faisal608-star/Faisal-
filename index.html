How I Built My Portfolio Website

July 2025

“I wanted my portfolio to feel clean, modern, and easy to navigate — just like the websites I love to build. I started with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, focusing on responsiveness and clean design. Choosing the right color palette and fonts was harder than I expected, but it taught me the value of visual harmony. The project cards section was my favorite part to design, and I used subtle hover effects to make it interactive. I also learned how to deploy my site so anyone can see it online. Overall, building my portfolio wasn’t just about showing what I can do — it was about learning, improving, and sharing my journey as a frontend developer.”
